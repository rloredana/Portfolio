Executive Performance Dashboard

An executive dashboard tracking key metrics, revenue trends, client performance, and insights through charts and visualizations, providing a clear overview of business performance toward targets.


Project Overview

Objective

Enable executives to monitor key performance indicators, track trends, and make data-driven decisions across multiple business areas, including revenue, client growth, and retention.

Audience

CEO, Executive Directors, Finance Teams, Marketing & Sales Leadership.

Technologies

Power BI for visualization, BigQuery for data warehousing, CRM & ERP systems, DSP platforms, internal databases, and ARIMA+ forecasting models.

Process

Data Sources & Preparation

  • Client & User Data: Platform tables loaded into BigQuery.
  • Campaign Data: DSP campaigns loaded into BigQuery for ad insights.
  • Financial Data: Transactions from ERP system consolidated in BigQuery.
  • Target Data: CRM targets table for sales tracking.
  • Data Preparation: Client segmentation (New, Returning, Existing), consolidated client-transaction view, consistency across platforms.

Dashboard Development & Design

  • KPI Cards: Revenue YTD, Gross Profit YTD, New Clients YTD, Target Achievement, Retention Rate.
  • Visualizations: Line Chart (Revenue vs Target), Bar Chart (Revenue by Segment), Ribbon Chart (Revenue by Client Segment/Channel), Decomposition Tree (Service & Package).
  • Forecasting: ARIMA+ models for Revenue and GP by month, client type, and segment.
  • Tools: Power BI integrated with BigQuery datasets for real-time insights.

Key Insights

  • Revenue and GP trends by client type highlight growth opportunities.
  • Client segmentation shows contribution of New, Returning, and Existing clients.
  • Forecasting identifies potential gaps in target achievement for proactive strategy.
  • Channel analysis shows which marketing channels drive revenue by segment.

Impact

  • Single source of truth for executive KPIs and trends.
  • Time saved: Eliminates manual data pulls from multiple platforms (CRM, ERP, DSP, platform databases) and Excel consolidation.
  • Supports data-driven decisions across finance, marketing, and sales.
  • Enables proactive client acquisition, retention, and revenue management.
  • Highlights high-value segments and performance gaps for strategic planning.
